Zhaochong An (安照崇)

I am an ELLIS PhD student at the University of Copenhagen, affiliated with Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence. I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Serge Belongie at the University of Copenhagen and Prof. Philip Torr at the University of Oxford. Prior to my PhD, I received the MSc in Computer Science from ETH Zurich working with Prof. Luc Van Gool.

My research interests are primarily computer vision, machine learning, and augmented reality. Specifically, my previous research has centered on the fascinating field of scene understanding, encompassing a wide array of scenarios such as image and video analysis as well as 2D and 3D environments. I would like to further delve into the 3D domain, with a focus on advancing our knowledge in scene construction and representation.
