Hello, I'm Zhaochong An

I am a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen, affiliated with the Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence, under the ELLIS program. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Serge Belongie. Prior to my PhD, I spent two wonderful years at ETH Zurich supervised by Prof. Luc Van Gool and received the MSc in Computer Science from ETH Zurich in 2023.

My research interests are primarily in multimodality and generative modeling. I have explored a broad range of scenarios within multimodal foundation models and video generation, including image and video analysis/generation, as well as 3D scene understanding. I have previously interned at Google and Meta.
