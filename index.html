Zhaochong An

I am a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen, affiliated with Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence, under ELLIS program. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Serge Belongie. Prior to my PhD, I spent two wonderful years at ETH Zurich supervised by Prof. Luc Van Gool and received the MSc in Computer Science from ETHz in 2023.

My research interests are primarily in computer vision and generative modeling. I have explored a broad range of scenarios within the field of multimodal foundation models and video generation, including image and video analysis/generation, as well as 3D scene understanding.
