Zhaochong An (安照崇)

I am a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen, affiliated with Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence, and the University of Oxford under ELLIS. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Serge Belongie and Prof. Philip Torr. Prior to my PhD, I spent two wonderful years at ETH Zurich supervised by Prof. Luc Van Gool and received the MSc in Computer Science from ETHz in 2023.

My research are primarily on computer vision and deep learning. Specifically, my previous research has centered on the fascinating field of scene understanding, encompassing a wide array of scenarios such as image and video analysis as well as 2D and 3D environments. Besides, I am also interested in improving and understanding the fondation models, and 3D scene representations.
